Discover Los Angeles

// Social Media Campaign Visuals

I created a mock Summer 2015 social media campaign for the Los Angeles Tourism and Convention Board and their LA tourism website, discoverlosangeles.com. I created a campaign targeting young professionals and millennials with imagery and copy that encourages the adventure and unique experiences they seek and LA can provide. The project features Facebook and Twitter cover photo designs with consistent messaging and branding in order to present a cohesive campaign across social channels.

Highlighted skills & tools: Adobe Photoshop, social media marketing, branding
