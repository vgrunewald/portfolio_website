Eggplant Custom Apps

// Visual Identity Creation

Eggplant Apps is a fictitious Bay Area tech start-up of my imagination. I created a complete suite of original visual identity elements for the brand, including logo, business card, letterhead and website homepage. I included a basic style guide to highlight my dedication to well thought-out and consistent branding. This project allowed me to apply my creative skills to the development a memorable brand identity that is just as fun as it is professional.

Highlighted skills & tools: Adobe Illustrator, logo design, branding
