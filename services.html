Original Web Content

As a busy small business owner, you don't always have a lot of time. With a background in professional writing, I can help write your website content so you don't have to. I can capture your unique brand voice and craft quality custom copy for your site that is appropriate in both content and tone. While I would not describe my content as pure SEO writing, know I will incorporate important keywords to your business throughout your custom copy.

Custom Web Designs

When a WordPress site isn't a good fit, it's best to start from scratch with a custom, one-of-a-kind web design. Using the latest and best in HTML, CSS and jQuery, I can design and develop a unique website just for your small business and your customers.

Website Maintenance

Allow me to provide additional technical features and assistance before and after your website launch. Add-ons include initial on-site SEO setup, website analytics setup and integration, website hosting and monthly website maintenance and content updates.

Branding & Logo Design

In need of a logo as well as a website? Let's work together to create a unique design that visually captures the essence of your brand and speaks to your target audience. Your new logo can be used on your website and beyond.
